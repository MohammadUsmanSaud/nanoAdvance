WHAT IS A NANO ADVANCE....?

Advance is a service brought to you by the Blue Label group, that allows you to recharge your prepaid electricity meter even when you're out of funds. Qualifying customers are able to take out an advance now and pay it back when they next recharge.

Customers are eligible according to a basic set of criteria which is then used to calculate the advance amount the client is eligible for based on their overall spend with the Blue Label group. Check out our FAQs for more info.
